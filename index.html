Revenue design

Monetization is built into the experience from day one.

You do not need to wait for massive scale. The site can earn through layered monetization even with focused niche traffic.

01 · Affiliate

Product and marketplace commissions

Link curated items to Amazon, brand programs, CJ, ShareASale, Rakuten, or direct merchant landing pages.

  • Best for product roundups and brand universe pages.
  • Works especially well for tech, home, beauty, and lifestyle.
  • High-intent pages can monetize with relatively low traffic.
02 · Sponsorship

Featured brand edits and paid placement

Once the visual quality and audience trust are established, brands can pay to appear in themed edits.

  • “Brand of the month” homepage slot.
  • Seasonal campaigns and launch showcases.
  • Newsletter sponsorship inventory.
03 · Premium

Members-only lists and private curation

Charge for access to premium buying guides, founder picks, investment-style brand reports, or concierge recommendations.

  • Collectors’ edits, luxury watch lists, workspace kits.
  • Private community or email membership layer.
  • Strong fit for design-conscious audiences.
